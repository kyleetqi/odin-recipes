Island Rice Bowl

Images are for illustration purposes only.

Description

Like pineapple on pizza? Good. You'll love this recipe. Hate pineapple on pizza? Too bad.

Each serving contains around 700 calories and 60 grams of protein.

Ingredients

  • 2.75 lbs chicken thigh or chicken breast, diced
  • 3 cups uncooked rice
  • Diced Pineapple

To Taste:

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper
  • Carrots
  • Oil
  • Curry Powder
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Whatever Other Seasonings You Want :)

Steps

  1. Add your rice to a rice cooker.
  2. Chop up your vegetables, and add them to a preheated oiled cast iron pan at medium high heat.
  3. Once the vegetables have been clarified, add your chicken and cooked at medium heat for around 10 minutes, flipping often.
  4. Once everything has finished cooking, combine in a large bowl and add your curry powder, soy sauce, and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Optional: Put everything back into a large wok and stir fry at high heat to lock in the flavour!
  6. Distribute into five equal servings.
  7. Dice up a generous amount of pineapple, and top off your dishes!

This meal lasts for up to four days in the fridge, and can be frozen. To reheat, microwave it for 2-3 minutes.
