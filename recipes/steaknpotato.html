Steak and Potatoes

Images are for illustration purposes only.

Description

Steak and potatoes. Bring this classic American dish to the comfort of your office.

Each serving contains around 750 calories and 65 grams of protein.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs of your choice of steak
  • 12 medium potatoes

To Taste:

  • Garlic
  • Garlic Aoili
  • Steak seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil
  • Whatever Other Seasonings You Want :)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. While the oven is preheating, wash and dice your potatoes.
  3. Transfer your diced potatoes to a large tray and toss it in oil and seasonings of your choice.
  4. Put the potatoes in the oven and set a timer for 50 minutes.
  5. While the potatoes are cooking, dice your steak and cook them on a preheated cast iron pan at medium high heat until a nice crust is developed.
  6. Add in your steak seasoning and garlic cloves to make it taste nice :)
  7. Once your steak and potatoes are done, distribute equally into five containers.

This meal lasts for up to four days in the fridge, and can be frozen. To reheat, microwave it for 2-3 minutes.
