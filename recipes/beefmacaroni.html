Beef and Macaroni

Images are for illustration purposes only.

Description

What's up with all these meal prep recipes that call for mac and cheese? Ditch the dairy and make this delicious Beef and Macaroni instead.

Each serving contains around 850 calories and 60 grams of protein.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Lean Ground Beef (15% or lower)
  • 1 lb dry macaroni

To Taste:

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper
  • Oil
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Whatever Other Seasonings You Want :)

Steps

  1. Start by heating a pot of salted water in preparation for your macaroni.
  2. While the water comes up to a boil, dice up your Onion, Garlic, and Bell Pepper.
  3. Add your diced vegetables into a preheated and oiled cast iron pan.
  4. While your vegetables are cooking, add your pasta into the boiling water.
  5. Once the vegetables become clarified, add in your ground beef.
  6. As the beef is cooking, add in tomato paste to taste and add in your chili powder, salt and pepper, and other seasonings of your choice.
  7. Take the macaroni off of the heat when it is slightly undercooked. It will finish cooking once you reheat it.
  8. Take your beef off of the heat, and mix it with the pasta.
  9. Distribute into five equal servings and enjoy!

This meal lasts for up to four days in the fridge, and can be frozen. To reheat, microwave it for 2-3 minutes.
